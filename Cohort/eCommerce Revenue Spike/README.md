# Ecommerce Cohort Analysis: Q1 2026 Acquisition Spike
   
   ## Overview
   Cohort analysis of The Look ecommerce dataset (BigQuery) to understand customer acquisition and retention patterns.
   
   ## Key Finding
   Q1 2026 cohort showed 26% higher acquisition vs Q4 2025, driven by search (+27%) and organic (+38%) traffic.
   
   ## Methodology
   - Cohort definition: Users grouped by signup quarter
   - Metric: Total sales by cohort and order quarter
   - Data: Filtered to Complete/Shipped/Processing orders only
   - Analysis: Traffic source breakdown to identify growth driver
   
   ## Results
   Search and organic channels drove Q1 2026 spike. Other channels remained flat.
   
   ## Next Steps
   - Monitor search/organic sustainability in Q2 2026
   - Analyze keyword performance to identify winning content
   - Compare LTV of search cohort vs other channels
